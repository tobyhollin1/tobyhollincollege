Founded in 2015 2 friends, Webster Devin and John Hot Beans, founded the company Hot Beans Web. Our company has been going strong after hitting the ground running after winning Tashkents 'Web Company of the Year' and Brasils 'Best Upcoming Company' awards in 2016.
Our company prides itself on developing the skills of junior web developers, as nearly all of our employees started of as juniors who we then molded into the successfull peopel who they are today.
With the future in mind, our company is switching to 50% wooden hardware, so that we will not need to recycle. All of the carpets in our building will be swapped ou for 100% reused and recycled mulch.
